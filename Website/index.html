Welcome to Dave's Forge – your premier destination for finely crafted medieval weapons!

At Dave's Forge, we uphold the timeless tradition of blacksmithing, where raw materials are meticulously transformed into works of art. Nestled in the heart of the countryside, our forge serves as the beating heart of our operation, where flames dance and hammers strike to bring medieval dreams to life.

Our Proud Work, Weapons!

Step into a world where history meets craftsmanship. Our collection of medieval weapons embodies the spirit of a bygone era, meticulously hand-forged by our skilled artisans. From mighty swords to fearsome axes, each weapon is a testament to our dedication to authenticity and quality.

Meet Dave

Dave, the master blacksmith behind it all, brings decades of experience and a passion for the craft to every piece created at the forge. His unwavering commitment to excellence ensures that each weapon bearing the Dave's Forge name is a masterpiece worthy of admiration.

The History Behind the Forge

Established in 1980, Dave's Forge has been a cornerstone of the community for generations. Our rich history is woven into every piece we create, honoring the legacy of those who came before us while forging a path toward the future of craftsmanship.
